Lasagna

The Secret Behind This Classic Lasagna Recipe

From what I can tell, the secret behind Alton's lasagna is the addition of a little sugar to the sauce and using a bit more cheese than most other recipes.

There are a few minor tweaks to his original recipe that I found necessary, for example adding a dash of wine vinegar to the sauce and changing the amount of Parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

  1. 2 teaspoons extra virgin olive oil
  2. 1 pound ground beef chuck
  3. 1/2 medium onion, diced (about 3/4 cup)
  4. 1/2 large bell pepper (green, red, or yellow), diced (about 3/4 cup)
  5. 2 cloves garlic, minced
  6. 1 (28-ounce)can good-quality tomato sauce
  7. 3 ounces tomato paste (half a 6-ounce can)
  8. 1 (14 ounce) can crushed tomatoes
  9. 2 tablespoons chopped fresh oregano, or 2 teaspoons
  10. dried oregano
  11. 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley (preferably flat leaf),
  12. 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  13. 1 pinch garlic powder and/or garlic salt
  14. 1 tablespoon red or white wine vinegar
  15. 1 tablespoon to 1/4 cup sugar (to taste, optional)
  16. Salt
